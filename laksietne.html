About Laks i Etne

The river Etne is a river more beautiful than you can imagine... Just about an hour drive from the city Haugesund and a few hours drive from the city Bergen you can find this lovely little river. Even if the river isn't the biggest, you can catch salmon as big as 18-19 kg's.
With it's 26 different fishing places you can choose the fishing you prefere.
