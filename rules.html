Rules for the fishing season 2019

  • Fishing for Salmon starts 15th. June (00:00) until 15th. August. The fishing license starts at 18:00 until 17:00 the next day. It's not allowed to kill the seabass.
  • Fishing equipment used in other rivers needs to be disinfected before use in the river. You can disinfect your equipment at Etne Camping
  • Fishing rod is the only allowed equipment for fishing and you are not allowed to leave the fishing rod while fishing. You are allowed to use flies, spinners, lures, worms and wobblers for fishing bait.
  • Salmon below 40cm and salmons that have stayed in the river over the winter needs to be set back into the river as gentle as possible. After 15th. July all salmons that are allow 100cm also needs to be put back into the river.
  • The fisher man needs to respect the rules for each area and it's not allowed to fish on other areas than you have a fishing license for.
  • When traveling over the farmer's land please respect their propperty and show respect to their land.
  • It's not allowed to put up a tent unless given permission from the owner of the land.
  • The fishing license is written in a name and its not allowed to sell, give or trade the fishing license with a 3rd. parti.
  • All fish that you have catch and release on you will have to report on the site.
  • When you buy a fishing license for the river you approve of the terms above.
